Nicola tesla

Biography

Nikola Tesla (1856-1943) was a Serbian-American inventor known for his contributions to the development of alternating current (AC) electricity, the invention of the Tesla coil, and his pioneering work in wireless communication and radio technology. He held around 300 patents and is celebrated for his significant impact on electrical engineering.
